Why We Created DeafHive?

Having access is important, but feeling part of the deaf community matters too.

  • Many events have interpreters booked, but deaf people don't always attend because they did not know about the event.
  • Finding BSL-friendly services, courses, and community groups can be difficult and time-consuming.
  • Deaf role models and success stories are rarely visible — we want to change that.

What You Will Find on DeafHive

Stories and BSL – Read and watch stories from the Deaf community and stay informed about Deaf issues.
